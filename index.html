About Me

I'm an upcoming senior at UCLA studying Data Theory and minoring in Data Science Engineering. I'm interested in machine learning, analytics, and applied AI, especially where technical work can be turned into something useful, interpretable, and actionable.

My work focuses on transforming messy, real-world datasets into structured analyses, predictive models, dashboards, and decision-ready insights. I've worked on healthcare, public health, and urban data projects, and I enjoy combining technical depth with clear communication.
